Who am I?

Jihan Basyah is a fashion journalist based in New York City. Currently undergoing her junior year in The New School, Jihan has written articles on fashion sustainability and covering fashion looks in and out of Parsons School of Design.

Since 2019, Jihan has worked as a fashion reporter for The New School Free Press. Some of her works can be found here.
